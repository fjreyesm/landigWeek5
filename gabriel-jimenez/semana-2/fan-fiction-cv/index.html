PROFILE

I am a handsome and charismatic cat with incredible agility and stamina obtained through my numerous encounters with a pesky mouse. A bluish-grey anthropomorphic cat with white colors on my belly, mouth, hands, and feet, I use a wide range of tricks to pursue my archenemy, however futile they might be. I have developed many an enemy over the years, ranging from mice to dogs and cats like myself. I am also known for my charming disposition, developing several love interests. Some of the things I enjoy the most are fresh fish, a warm bowl of milk, catching mice, and spending time with my family.

OBJECTIVE

Following my basic instincts and desire to remain a house cat, I am always looking for new methods to improve my mice-catching efficiency. I plan to embark on a diplomatic journey to unite cats and dogs under one faction to bring down the true enemy: mice.
