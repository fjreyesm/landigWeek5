The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

prep time

45 Min

ingirdients

  1. 1/2 cup (3 sticks) butter, softened
  2. 1 cup brown sugar
  3. 1 cup granulated sugar
  4. 1 Tbl vanilla instant pudding powder
  5. 2 Tbl milk
  6. 2 Tbl vanilla extract
  7. 2 eggs
  8. 4 cups all purpose flour
  9. tsp baking soda
  10. 2 tsp salt
  11. cups chocolate chips
  12. cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  • butter and sugars together until light and fluffy
  • in pudding mix milk and vanilla extract.
  • Beat in eggs.
  • dry ingredients and stir until combined.
  • in chocolate chips and nuts.
  • 1 1/2 inch balls of dough 2 inches apart on an un greased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.
