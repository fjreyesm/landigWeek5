The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:by: My Grandma

Prep Time:

45 Min

Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/2 cup (3 sticks) butter, softened
  2. 1 cup brown sugar
  3. 1 cup granulated sugar
  4. 1 Tbl vanilla instant pudding powder
  5. 2 eggs
  6. 4 cups all purpose flour
  7. 2 tsp baking soda
  8. 1/2 tsp salt
  9. 4 cups chocolate chips
  10. 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Preparation:

      oven to 350 degrees
